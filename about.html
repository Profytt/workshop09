A few things I enjoy to maintain my work life balance

Cooking

I have always enjoyed cooking. Finding new recipies to try and different methods always kept me interested in learning more. With my work history, I have picked up a multitude of skills I can use every day.
I love the feeling I get when others enjoy a dish I worked hard on. Wheather it's grilling, baking, or sauteing, I'm sure I can make something that would put a smile on anyone's face.

Tech

I think my love for tech comes from the fact I was born in the early 90's. Because of that, I have lived through the tech boom that came in that decade.
My dad was a person who also had an interest in learning about new tech. He captivated me when the Nintendo 64 came out and i would always sit and watch him play Star Fox. Or when portable DVD players first came out and everyone asked me what it was because no one has seen one before. That interest at a young age has always kept me in the loop to learn about new applications and devices.

Music

Music has always been a big part of my life. When I was young, I learned to play piano and also played in the school band. Im the band, I learned to play all brass intruments as well as some percussion. That knowledge at a young age taught me music theory, which helped me in my early 20's as I started to produce music.
My listening taste mostly includes hHip Hop, but i also listen to some R&B, Neo Soul, and Lofi for relaxation. I always listen to analyse the music and beat selectoin to try to understand the process the producer went through to make the track. From samples to producing a song from scratch, if a song catches my ear I'll listen to it multiple times in a row to try to understand it.
